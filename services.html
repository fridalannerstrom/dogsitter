Our dogcare services

We offer three different types of services for those in need of dog care: daycare, overnight stays, and drop-in walks.

Dog Daycare

Full-day care for your dog while you are away.

At Sunne Dog Daycare, we believe that every dog deserves a fun-filled day! Whether you're off to work or running errands, your dog can enjoy a day packed with activities, socialization, and lots of love in a safe environment. Drop them off in the morning and pick them up at your convenience.

250 kr/day

Duration: Between 7 AM and 10 PM
Walks: Daily walks in the nearby forest
Activities: Playtime, socialization with other dogs, and lots of outdoor fun.
Areas: Safe and secure fenced garden and indoor areas
Food: Bring your dog's own food
Other: Feel free to bring your dog's favorite toys and treats

Overnight Stay

A comfortable home-away-from-home.

Whether you're away for just one night or an extended stay of several weeks, Sunne Dogcare offers your dog a safe, warm, and loving environment where they will feel truly at home. Our Overnight Stay service guarantees that while you're away, your dog enjoys a cozy, stress-free experience, complete with personalized care and all the comforts of home.

500 kr/night

Duration: Completely flexible
Walks: Daily walks in the nearby forest
Activities: Playtime, socialization with other dogs, and lots of outdoor fun.
Areas: Safe and secure fenced garden and indoor areas
Food: Bring your dog's own food
Sleeping: Sleep in our bed or in a cozy dog bed right next to us.
Other: Feel free to bring your dog's favorite toys, treats and blanket.

Dog walking Drop-In

A fun and energizing time for your dog.

If your schedule is packed and you don't have time to give your dog the exercise they need, our drop-in walking service is the perfect solution. We'll take your dog on a group walk through the beautiful woods, giving them the chance to stretch their legs, socialize with other dogs, and enjoy the fresh air. Whether it's for one walk or a regular routine, we've got you covered!

250 kr/drop-in

Duration: Between 7 AM and 4 PM
Walks: At least 2 hour walk in nearby forest
Activities: Playtime, socialization with other dogs, and lots of outdoor fun.
Areas: Safe and secure fenced garden and indoor areas
Food: No food needed

FAQ

What are your requirements for my dog?
I ask that your dog is friendly towards other dogs, as we will have multiple dogs here at the same time. Your dog also needs to be comfortable around Children. It’s a bonus if your dog is used to cats, we have three cats at home.

Do we meet before I book?
Yes, I meet all new dog owners and their dogs before the first stay. This allows us to get to know each other and ensures that your dog feels comfortable around my dogs before staying here alone. This meeting takes about an hour.

What dogs do you have?
I have two dogs of my own: a 1-year-old labradoodle, who is full of energy and very playful, and a 10-year-old maltipoo, who is calm and well-used to being around other dogs. Both of my dogs are social and love the company of other dogs.

How many dogs do you take care of at the same time?
I take care of a maximum of 2 dogs at a time, in addition to my own, to make sure every dog receives the attention and care they need.

What if my dog doesn’t get along with other dogs?
Unfortunately, we can’t accommodate dogs that show aggressive behavior or have difficulty tolerating others. While it’s okay if the dogs don’t become best friends, they need to coexist peacefully. I prioritize the well-being of my own dogs and the others in my care, so aggressive dogs won’t be a good fit.
