We offer three different types of services for those in need of dog care: daycare, overnight stays, and drop-in walks.

Dog Daycare

Full-day care for your dog while you are away.

At Sunne Dog Daycare, we believe that every dog deserves a fun-filled day! Whether you're off to work or running errands, your dog can enjoy a day packed with activities, socialization, and lots of love in a safe environment. Drop them off in the morning and pick them up at your convenience.

250 kr/day

Duration: Between 7 AM and 10 PM
Walks: Daily walks in the nearby forest
Activities: Playtime, socialization with other dogs, and lots of outdoor fun.
Areas: Safe and secure fenced garden and indoor areas
Food: Bring your dog's own food
Other: Feel free to bring your dog's favorite toys and treats
